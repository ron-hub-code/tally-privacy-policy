Privacy Policy for Tally

Effective date: 2026-04-09

Tally is a simple counter app designed to help you count things quickly on your device.

Information We Collect

Tally stores your counters and app preferences locally on your device so the app can work properly.

Tally may display advertisements. Advertising services may collect limited device or usage data as described by their own privacy policies in order to provide and measure ads.

Tally does not require you to create an account.

How Your Information Is Used

Your local app data is used only to provide the core app functionality, such as saving your counters and app state on your device.

Advertising-related data, where applicable, may be processed by the ad provider to serve and measure ads.

Data Sharing

Tally does not sell your personal data.

Tally may use third-party advertising services, and those services may process limited technical data according to their own privacy terms.

Data Storage

Your counters are stored locally on your device unless a future version explicitly adds cloud features.

Children

Tally is not specifically directed to children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will be reflected by a new effective date.
